WELCOME TO
SUN Education
SUN Education is a group of institutions dedicated to providing meaningful learning and life experiences to those who don’t need wings to fly. Our institution has been thriving since 2004, with a perfectly sound model consisting of corporate as well as the non-profit community. SUN Education is proudly affiliated with Mohan Lal Sukhadia University (MLSU) and offers degree courses such as B. Com, BBA, BCA, etc. In order to find a way forward, SUN Education is working for the development of the underprivileged with the association of various state governments as well as with the Government of PAN India. We help identify the most vulnerable people in India and beyond so that you can help accelerate their growth.

Director's Message
SUN Education is passionate about grooming leaders, who became a champion in their own field and a better person. The group of institutions currently provides regular courses, distant learning programs, NGO activities for enhancing the quality of education, skill development activities, etc. The advanced curriculum at SUN Education helps students excel in their academic years to come. The study curriculum is articulated to let students master the fundamentals of the program, as well as to develop practical knowledge and implementation of things down the road. We, as an institution, are continuously working to empower students across India in association with the government so that they can get their right to education. My team and I are vigorously maximising the resources to manage the cost of learning per child or student.
